# KalTrac 2.0 — Luxury Leather Design System

## Bespoke Craftsmanship UI

### Visual Philosophy:

- **Leather Textures**: High-resolution dark leather patterns with subtle grain.
- **Saddle Stitching**: Custom SVG paths and dashed borders simulating hand-stitched leather.
- **Brass & Gold Accents**: Luxurious metallic highlights using `#c5a055` and radial glows.
- **Buckle Headers**: Unique section headers that resemble leather belts with metallic buckles.
- **Inset Depth**: Realistic debossing effects using multiple inner shadows.

### Theme Variations:

- **Classic Brown**: Traditional mahogany and tobacco tones.
- **British Racing Green**: Deep emerald leather with high contrast.
- **Blush Rose**: Soft pink leather with rose-gold metallic accents.

### Premium Components:

- **The Tracker Ring**: Gold-embossed progress circle with internal calorie counting.
- **Safety Panels**: Ingredient alerts styled as official stamped warnings.
- **Analytics Scrolls**: Data visualisations rendered with Recharts but integrated into the hand-crafted UI.

### Technical Implementation:

- **Pure CSS**: No heavy UI libraries; 100% bespoke `app.css`.
- **Responsive**: Fluid layouts that maintain the premium feel from Desktop to iPhone.
- **Performance**: GPU-accelerated glows and transforms for 60fps interaction.
